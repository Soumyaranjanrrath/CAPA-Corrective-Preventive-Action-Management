# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name
CAPA (Corrective & Preventive Action) Management

---

## 1. Introduction

The CAPA (Corrective & Preventive Action) Management application is designed to help organizations manage issues through a structured and standardized process. It enables teams to identify problems, investigate root causes, implement corrective and preventive actions, obtain necessary approvals, verify effectiveness, and close CAPA records in a controlled manner.

The application improves accountability, process visibility, and compliance by providing a centralized platform for managing the complete CAPA lifecycle.

---

## 2. Business Problem

Many organizations manage corrective and preventive actions using emails, spreadsheets, or manual documentation. This results in:

- Inconsistent issue tracking
- Lack of ownership and accountability
- Delayed approvals
- Missed corrective actions
- Limited visibility into CAPA progress
- Difficulty during compliance audits
- Inefficient reporting and monitoring

A centralized ServiceNow application addresses these challenges by standardizing the CAPA process and automating key business workflows.

---

## 3. Business Objectives

The application aims to:

- Centralize CAPA records in a single system
- Standardize the issue management process
- Improve accountability through task assignments
- Automate approvals and notifications
- Track corrective and preventive actions
- Monitor CAPA progress and status
- Improve compliance and audit readiness
- Provide dashboards and reports for management

---

## 4. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|-------------|----------------|
| Employee | Reports issues requiring CAPA |
| CAPA Coordinator | Reviews, manages, and monitors CAPA records |
| Investigator | Performs root cause analysis |
| Manager | Reviews and approves action plans |
| Action Owner | Implements assigned corrective and preventive actions |
| QA Team | Verifies action effectiveness before closure |
| System Administrator | Configures and maintains the application |

---

## 5. Project Scope

### In Scope

- Issue reporting
- CAPA record management
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective action planning
- Preventive action planning
- Assignment of action owners
- Approval workflow
- Email notifications
- Verification process
- CAPA closure
- Reports and dashboards

### Out of Scope

- Third-party system integrations
- AI-based recommendations
- Mobile application
- Predictive risk analysis
- Multi-company support
- Advanced audit management

---

## 6. Business Requirements

The system shall:

- Allow users to report issues requiring CAPA.
- Maintain complete CAPA records.
- Support root cause analysis.
- Record corrective and preventive action plans.
- Assign actions to responsible users.
- Route records for manager approval.
- Notify users of important updates.
- Track CAPA progress through defined states.
- Verify completed actions before closure.
- Generate reports and dashboards for monitoring.

---

## 7. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Standardizes the CAPA lifecycle.
- Improves visibility into issue resolution.
- Reduces manual tracking activities.
- Increases accountability through ownership assignment.
- Automates approvals and notifications.
- Enables management reporting using dashboards.
- Provides a practical and scalable ServiceNow solution.
